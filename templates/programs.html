In the coming months, CSTUY plans to roll out numerous programs including:

Classes

CSTUY plans on offering a range of after school classes to middle and high school students. These will range form short form single session classes to multi week classes and will be offered to both students new to computer science as well as to those with experience. All courses will be developed as a partnership between CSTUY, its teachers, and industry professionals to insure that students develop new problem solving skills as well as a practical set of tools.

We hope to offer our first classes in the coming months.

Internships

Building on informal arrangements that have existed for years between CSTUY's board members and the Tech Industry, CSTUY is developing a structured internship program for students. This will entail placing student intern at local tech companies as well as providing structure for the internships that will include group activities, lectures, and workshops.

We hope to offer our first internships early this spring.

Lectures

By extending current informal relationships, CSTUY will host regular lectures on technology related topics and make these lectures available to high school and middle school students.

Summer Programs

Summer programs will allow us to work with students and teachers over an extended period of time. Plans include:

  • A month long introduction to computer science
  • CS / Tech industry boot camp from existing programmers
  • CS Bootcamp / Internship combo

Teacher Training

One of the biggest needs facing CS Education today is the lack of qualified teachers. CSTUY members have had numerous successes in training computer science teachers and building computer science programs and classes.

CSTUY is planning to offer numerous professional development opportunities to educators ranging from individual lectures to opportunities for new teachers to intern at our programs

CSTUY will also work with schools and teachers to develop computer science programs appropriate for their student populations.

More to come

CSTUY also plans on offering mentoring, online content, curriculum and more. Make sure to join our mailing list and stay tuned.
